Brief History

Ping pong started in England as an after dinner game. It became popular in 1901 and recieved standardized rules once the ITTF was founded in 1926. This was when the rules were set and ping pong became an official sport. Ping pong is now played in many throunamets around the world and became an olympic sport in 1988. Ping pong is increasing in poplularity for the younger generation from youtubers such as the ponfininity channel and for me Adam Bobrow.
